About Us

Match & Score was created by AlleyLabs, a studio known for combining classic gameplay with innovative strategies. The goal was to create a football-themed puzzle game that brings both excitement and mental challenge.

The game's unique blend of match-3 mechanics and football management encourages strategic thinking while still delivering thrilling sports moments.

The idle reward system ensures that players consistently progress and build stronger teams, even while offline.

The addition of live PvP matches offers players the opportunity to compete with friends and climb global leaderboards.

Our mission is to provide both football fans and puzzle lovers with an engaging, rewarding experience that keeps them coming back for more.

FAQ

  • What makes Match & Score different from other puzzle games?
    Match & Score combines classic match-3 puzzle gameplay with strategic football tactics. Building a balanced team, forming the right combinations, and making key decisions is essential to success.
  • How does the idle rewards system work?
    While you're offline, your team continues earning resources. When you return, you'll receive these rewards to improve your squad.
  • Are the PvP matches real-time?
    Yes! Players can engage in real-time PvP duels against opponents worldwide, competing for rewards and leaderboard rankings.
  • What should I do if I'm stuck on a challenging level?
    Focus on creating special tile combinations and consider upgrading your team members to improve their performance in tougher matches.
  • Is Match & Score free to play?
    Yes, the game is free to download and play. However, there are optional in-game purchases to enhance your experience.
  • Can I play without an internet connection?
    Yes! While PvP matches require internet, you can enjoy offline levels and still earn idle rewards during breaks.
